## OUTPUT RULES — READ BEFORE WRITING

Your response MUST:
- Output the exact marker `---BEGIN_CV---` on its own line immediately before the CV
- Start the CV immediately after that marker with `# {Full Name}`
- Contain ONLY reasoning (if any) before the marker, and ONLY the CV after it
- End after the last certification — no closing remarks

Your response MUST NOT contain:
- Step numbers or step headers (STEP 1, STEP 2, etc.) after the ---BEGIN_CV--- marker
- Reasoning notes, decision summaries, or process commentary after the marker
- "Here is your CV" or any variant
- Any text between ---BEGIN_CV--- and # {Full Name}

The marker serves as a clean separator so reasoning can be captured separately.
Even if you have no reasoning to output, the marker must still appear.
