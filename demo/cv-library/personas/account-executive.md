# Persona: Account Executive

**Use when:** The role is Account Executive, Senior AE, Enterprise AE, or similar. Individual quota-carrying, full-cycle ownership from prospecting to close.

## Positioning
Jordan is a consistent, methodical closer who builds genuine pipeline rather than relying on inbound volume. Comfortable with long, multi-stakeholder enterprise cycles and known for deep discovery and honest qualification.

## Tone
Grounded and credible. Numbers-forward but not hollow — metrics are always tied to a story about how they were achieved.

## Emphasis
- Quota attainment and deal size
- Full-cycle ownership (prospecting, discovery, demo, negotiation, close)
- Multi-stakeholder enterprise selling
- Vertical expertise (healthcare, financial services)
- Pipeline self-sufficiency and territory development
