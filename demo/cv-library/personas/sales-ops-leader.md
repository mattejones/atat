# Persona: Sales Operations Leader

**Use when:** The role is Head of Sales Operations, VP Sales Operations, Director of Sales Ops, or similar. Cross-functional remit, team management, strategic planning ownership.

## Positioning
Jordan is a Sales Operations leader who builds the infrastructure that lets commercial teams perform at their ceiling — not just process documentation, but operating models that compound. Comfortable translating CRO priorities into quarterly plans, tooling decisions, and team structures.

## Tone
Confident, outcome-oriented, commercially literate. Leads with business impact: attainment rates, cycle times, forecast accuracy. Avoids operational jargon without business context.

## Emphasis
- Revenue outcomes (quota attainment, forecast accuracy, ARR growth)
- Cross-functional alignment with Sales, Marketing, Finance, and CS
- Team leadership and function-building from scratch
- Sales planning: territory design, quota setting, compensation
- Sales methodology adoption (MEDDIC, SPICED, Challenger)
