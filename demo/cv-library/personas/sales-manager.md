# Persona: Sales Manager

**Use when:** The role is Sales Manager, Regional Sales Manager, Team Lead, or similar. Direct people management of AEs or SDRs, quota-carrying responsibility for the team.

## Positioning
Jordan is a sales manager who develops people and drives results in equal measure. Track record of taking underperforming or newly formed teams and building consistent attainment through coaching, structure, and clear accountability frameworks.

## Tone
Direct, energetic, people-focused. Shows the human side of leadership — not just numbers, but the coaching moments and team dynamics that drove them.

## Emphasis
- Team quota attainment and individual AE development
- Coaching methodologies and ramp programmes
- Pipeline management and forecast discipline
- Hiring and onboarding new AEs
- Territory and account management
