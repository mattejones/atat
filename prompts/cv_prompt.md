### Example 8 — Third-person name reference in earlier experience

WEAK: "Earlier technical support roles at TIBCO (2011–2015), where Matt served as the internal subject matter expert for ActiveMatrix BPM, and at Sun Microsystems/Oracle (2007–2011), where Matt built his Java debugging foundations."

STRONG: "Earlier technical support roles at TIBCO (2011–2015) — served as the internal subject matter expert for ActiveMatrix BPM 1.0, owning documentation, escalations, and training from scratch — and at Sun Microsystems/Oracle (2007–2011), where foundational Java debugging and enterprise integration depth was established."

WHY: The CV is written in implied first person. The candidate's name must never appear in the body of the CV — not in bullets, not in summary paragraphs, not in the earlier experience section. Drop the name entirely and let the implied subject carry the sentence.
